<objective>
## Goal
Add two new symbol attributes — `tested: bool` (does a test call/import this symbol?) and `centrality: float` (PageRank score on the call graph) — to the schema, compute them in new ingestion phases, and surface centrality in hybrid search ranking.

## Purpose
These attributes make axon genuinely intelligent: `tested` flags symbols without test coverage (dead + untested = refactor candidates), and `centrality` lets `axon_query` rank important symbols higher instead of treating all matches equally. Together they transform axon from an index into a quality-aware intelligence layer.

## Output
- Modified: model.py, kuzu_backend.py, kuzu_schema.py, kuzu_bulk.py, kuzu_search.py, pipeline.py
- New: ingestion/test_coverage.py, ingestion/centrality.py
- New tests for both phases
</objective>
